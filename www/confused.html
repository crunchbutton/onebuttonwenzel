What is OneButtonWenzel.com?

OneButtonWenzel.com sells the famous Wenzel sandwich made at Alpha Delta Pizza in New Haven Connecticut. It is a site built and run by Yale students and devoted Wenzel fans, and endorsed by the owners of Alpha Delta Pizza.

What is a Wenzel?

A Wenzel is a glorious sandwich - usually consisting of buffalo chicken, cheese, lettuce, tomato, and mayo on a toasty bun. However, you can buy a Wenzel anyway you like it - with ranch, blue cheese, bacon, extra sauce, eggplant instead of chicken, ... and the list goes on. A standard Wenzel costs $8.75.

What can I expect when getting Wenzeled?

Upon clicking the 'Wenzel Me' button, an order will be placed with Alpha Delta, who will deliver you your Wenzel(s) in approximately half an hour. If paying with credit card, you must complete the checkout process before the order is placed. If you would like to pickup your order at Alpha Delta, just type 'pickup' in the delivery box.

How can I ensure a smooth order process?

If you need to cancel an order, please do Alpha Delta a favor and give them a call within a couple of minutes of placing the order - they will start making your sandwich right away so don't delay. Also, please enter your correct phone number. The delivery guy needs to let you know when he arrives! Any orders with invalid phone numbers or addresses will be rejected.
